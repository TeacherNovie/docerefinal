About Docere English Online

Docere English Online is the heartfelt creation of Teacher Novie, a dedicated Science teacher from the Philippines who, after three years of teaching, found her true calling in the world of online English education. Fueled by a passion for making learning accessible, enjoyable, and meaningful, Teacher Novie took a bold step in 2023 and established an office for online teachers — a vibrant hub where educators with diverse talents unite under one mission: to deliver fun, interactive, and fulfilling lessons to learners everywhere.

With the strong support and partnership of Angel Bert Pontemayor, the office quickly blossomed into a thriving educational community. Their teamwork and shared vision made the office not just a workplace, but a lively center of inspiration and growth. From its humble beginnings to its continued success into 2025, the Docere English Online office stands as a testament to what passionate educators can achieve when they work together for a greater cause.

At Docere English Online, every lesson is crafted with care, every student is valued, and every learning journey is celebrated. Join us and experience the joy of learning with a team that believes in making education both transformative and truly enjoyable.

Meet Your Teacher

My name is Novie, your Science and English teacher. I am a passionate educator with a Bachelor’s degree in Secondary Education, majoring in General Science. In addition to my academic qualifications, I hold a TEFL (Teaching English as a Foreign Language) certificate, which has equipped me with specialized skills for teaching English to non-native speakers. Over the past three years, I have gained extensive experience in online teaching, where I have developed a keen understanding of effective virtual teaching strategies and tools. My background in general science, combined with my TEFL certification and online teaching experience, enables me to create engaging, informative, and interactive lessons that cater to diverse learning needs and environments. See you in my class and have a great day!
